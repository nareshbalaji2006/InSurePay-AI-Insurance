# 🚀 InSurePay – AI Income Protection System

InSurePay is an **AI-powered, fraud-resilient income protection system with zero-touch claims for gig workers**.
It is designed to safeguard delivery partners from income loss caused by environmental disruptions like heavy rain, extreme heat, and poor air quality — while ensuring fairness through trust-based validation.

## 💡 Inspiration

Gig economy workers rely on daily earnings, but external conditions such as weather and pollution can disrupt their ability to work.

While existing parametric insurance solutions focus on payouts, they often fail in real-world scenarios due to:

* Fraudulent claims
* GPS spoofing
* Lack of trust validation

We built **InSurePay** to address these gaps — combining automation with **fraud-aware decision making**.

---

## 🧠 What It Does

InSurePay is a **smart income protection system** that:

* Monitors real-time environmental conditions
* Calculates dynamic insurance premiums
* Detects disruption events automatically
* Triggers instant payouts (zero manual claims)
* Prevents fraud using trust-based validation

---

## ⚙️ How We Built It

* **Frontend:** Antigravity + HTML, CSS, JavaScript
* **Backend:** Flask (Python)
* **Core Logic:** Risk scoring + trigger-based automation
* **Deployment:** GitHub Pages (frontend hosting)

---

## 🧩 Core Features

### 🔹 Dynamic Premium Calculation

Premiums are calculated based on environmental risk:

* Low Risk → ₹20
* Medium Risk → ₹40
* High Risk → ₹60

---

### 🔹 Multi-Trigger Detection

The system detects disruptions using:

* 🌧️ Rain (rain > 70)
* 🌡️ Heat (temperature > 42°C)
* 🌫️ AQI (AQI > 200)

---

### 🔹 Zero-Touch Claim System

* Fully automated claim triggering
* No manual user intervention
* Instant payout simulation

---

### 🔹 Fraud Detection & Trust Score

InSurePay goes beyond traditional systems by addressing **real-world deployment challenges**:

Trust Score is calculated using:

* Movement consistency
* Claim behavior
* Device validation

This ensures fair payouts and prevents misuse.

---

### 🔹 Claim History & Analytics

* Tracks last claim
* Displays earnings protected
* Shows total claims
